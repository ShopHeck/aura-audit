# App Store Review Notes Draft

Aura Audit is an entertainment app that generates fictional, humorous vibe reports from user-submitted selfies.

It does not evaluate attractiveness, identity, protected traits, health, psychology, intelligence, or real-world character. Users are instructed to upload only themselves or images they have permission to use.

The app has no public feed, public voting, leaderboard, or hot-or-not ranking. Selfies are processed only to generate the result and are not publicly posted or stored by default.

Premium unlocks use StoreKit 2. Ads are shown through AdMob only to non-premium users and are not targeted using selfie, face, or audit data.

A demo result is available in the app for review under "Use Demo Result for App Review".
