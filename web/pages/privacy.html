Privacy Policy

Last updated: Replace before launch.

Aura Audit is an entertainment app that generates fictional vibe reports from selfies.

Selfies

Selfies are used to generate an Aura Audit result. By default, raw selfies are processed transiently and are not stored after the request is completed.

Stored data

We may store an anonymous installation ID, audit mode, generated scores, generated text, timestamps, entitlement status, and safety reports.

Advertising

Free users may see ads. We do not use selfies, face data, or audit text to target advertising.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled through Apple StoreKit.

Contact

Email support@auraaudit.app for privacy questions or deletion requests.
